About Us

Vivekanand Education Society's Institute of Technology, also known as VESIT was established in the year 1984 and is located in Mumbai, Maharashtra. It is affiliated with the University of Mumbai and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). It offers various specialisations in Engineering courses at UG and PG levels and also offers MCA programme in Computer Applications. All of its UG programmes are accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA).

    Frequently Asked Questions

    • Q. Does the Vivekanand Education Society’s Institute of Technology have a hostel?:

      A. Yes, it has in campus boys' and girls' hostel with high-speed Wi-Fi internet connectivity.

    • Q. How can I get admission in the Vivekanand Education Society’s Institute of Technology?

      A. Admissions in UG programme are on the basis MH CET and JEE Main scorecard and for PG courses, admissions are on the basis of GATE score. To be eligible for admissions, the candidates must have qualified the entrance exam and have a valid scorecard. After this, all eligible students are called for a counselling round to fill up the choices. On the basis of choices and rank seats are allotted.

    • Q. Is there any scholarship at Vivekanand Education Society’s Institute of Technology?

      A. Yes, the college offers various scholarships to the students on the basis of academic records, financial background etc.
